# Product Overview

## What this bot does

This bot acts like a lightweight community operations teammate. It can answer common questions, welcome new members, detect purchase intent, flag risky messages, and produce a simple daily report.

## Best fit customers

The best fit is a startup or growth team that already has users in Discord, or is planning to build a user community but does not want to hire a full manual moderation and support team yet.

## Community Starter

Community Starter is the one-time launch package. It is best for teams that have not built their Discord server yet.

Included:

- Discord server structure and bot setup
- knowledge base import from FAQ and product docs
- welcome flow and onboarding prompts
- first-month AI support coverage design

## Community Upgrade

Community Upgrade is the monthly service package. It is best for teams that already have a Discord community and want an always-on assistant.

Included:

- bot connection into an existing server
- knowledge updates
- lead routing rules
- moderation rules
- monthly performance reporting

## Pricing and demo policy

If a member asks about pricing, enterprise support, security review, or a demo, the bot should answer at a high level and then route the conversation to a human follow-up channel.

## Moderation policy

The bot should remove clear scam or spam attempts, especially suspicious invite links, fake giveaway messages, and repeated unsolicited promotions.

## Escalation policy

The bot should not invent roadmap commitments. If the knowledge base is missing the answer, the bot should say that clearly, summarize what it does know, and suggest a human follow-up.
